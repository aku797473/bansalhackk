# Smart Kisan Premium Design System

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetics**: Premium, modern, clean, and data-driven.
- **Vibe**: Trustworthy agricultural technology meets cutting-edge SaaS.
- **Effects**: Glassmorphism, subtle gradients, soft shadows, and smooth transitions.

## Colors
- **Primary**: Indigo-600 (#4f46e5) for actions and brand identity.
- **Secondary**: Emerald-600 (#10b981) for growth, health, and success.
- **Accent**: Violet-600 (#7c3aed) for intelligence and premium features.
- **Background**: 
  - Light: #F8FAFC (Slate 50) with subtle grid patterns.
  - Dark: #0B1120 (Slate 950) with deep blue undertones.
- **Surface**: Glassy white (#ffffffcc) or Sleek dark (#1e293bcc) with backdrop-blur.

## Typography
- **Primary**: 'Inter', sans-serif (Clean, professional).
- **Secondary**: 'Outfit', sans-serif (Modern, friendly).
- **Weights**: 
  - 400: Regular
  - 600: Semi-bold for UI labels.
  - 800: Extra-bold for headings.

## Shapes
- **Bento Grid**: 2xl (1rem) to 3xl (1.5rem) corner radius.
- **Buttons**: Rounded-xl (0.75rem).
- **Inputs**: Rounded-lg (0.5rem).

## Interactions
- **Hover**: Subtle lift (translate-y-1) and scale (1.02).
- **Transitions**: 300ms ease-in-out.
- **Feedback**: Soft haptic-like animations on click.
